Detailed Description

Wildcard pattern matching (* and ?)

Simple wildcard matching with * (zero or more chars) and ? (one char). Faster than regex for simple patterns like "*.log" or "test_?.txt".

Performance:
  • Matching: O(n) with backtracking for *
  • Memory: No additional state
