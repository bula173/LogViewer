Detailed Description

Compact browser-style inline search bar for the events table.

Hidden by default. Show with FocusInput() (typically triggered by Ctrl+F). Emits SearchChanged whenever the text or case-sensitivity flag changes. The owner is responsible for calling SetMatchInfo() to update the counter.
